Lord Venkateswara (Sanskrit: वेङ्कटेश्वर, IAST: Vēṅkaṭēśvara), also known as Śrīnivāsa,
Bālājī, Vēṅkaṭa, Venkata Ramana, Vēṅkaṭāchalapati, Tirupati Timmappa, Ezhumalaiyaan,
Edukondalavaadu, Aapadamokkulavadu and Govindha, is a form of the Hindu god Maha Vishnu.
Lord Venkateswara is the presiding deity of Tirumala Venkateswara Temple located in Tirupati,
Andhra Pradesh, India.
